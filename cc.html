Calorie Deficit Calculator uses the BMI-based body fat estimation formula:

For males: 1.20 × BMI + 0.23 × Age - 16.2 | For females: 1.20 × BMI + 0.23 × Age - 5.4

Fat mass is calculated as Estimated Body Fat % × Weight, and 1 kg of fat is assumed to equal 7,700 kcal.

All calculations are estimates and should not replace professional medical advice.
